# Answer Offer Summary

## Core Positioning

Answer is not an AI novelty product.
It is a revenue recovery and inbound conversion system for businesses that lose money when calls go unanswered or follow-up is slow.

## Core Promise

We stop businesses from losing revenue to missed calls by answering every call, booking appointments, and following up instantly.

## Core Problems Solved

- missed inbound calls
- delayed speed-to-lead
- weak follow-up
- front-desk inconsistency
- labor-cost pressure
- revenue leakage from unhandled demand

## Why It Matters

For many local and service businesses, missed calls are not just missed conversations.
They are missed jobs, missed appointments, and missed cash flow.
