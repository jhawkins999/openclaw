# Roofing Answer Lead Qualification

## Strong-Fit Signals

- meaningful inbound call volume
- recent storm / hail / weather-driven demand
- active ad spend or lead generation already in market
- office complaints about phones or callback load
- owner mentions losing jobs to faster responders
- after-hours or weekend call leakage

## Medium-Fit Signals

- owner is growth-oriented but unclear on current leakage
- small office team handling calls manually
- seasonal spikes but no clear system for overflow

## Weak-Fit Signals

- very low inbound volume
- owner insists calls are never missed and speed is not an issue
- no urgency, no growth push, no visible call pressure
- business too small / too early to feel the problem yet

## Qualification Questions

- What happens when call volume spikes after storms?
- How quickly are new inbound callers usually contacted?
- What happens to after-hours calls right now?
- Have you ever looked at how many calls are missed or how often callbacks lag?
- If a lead calls two or three roofers, how often do you think the first response wins?

## Goal

Qualify for pain, urgency, and economics — not just curiosity.
