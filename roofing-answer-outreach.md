# Roofing Answer Outreach Pack

Last updated: 2026-03-23

## Goal

Generate conversations with roofing companies by leading with a simple, urgent business problem:

**missed calls = missed inspections = missed jobs**

Do not lead with broad AI positioning.
Lead with revenue leakage during storm-driven demand spikes.

---

## Core Outreach Principles

- Keep it short
- Lead with the business pain
- Make the timing relevant
- Sound local and practical, not "agency"
- Do not over-explain the product in the first touch
- Sell the conversation, not the whole system

---

## Core Angle

After hail, wind, or storm events, roofing companies often experience:
- sudden call spikes
- overloaded office staff
- delayed callbacks
- after-hours lead leakage
- lost inspections to faster competitors

Answer helps reduce that leakage by answering calls, booking inspections, and following up instantly.

## Objection Handling

### "We already have office staff."
That’s fine.
This is not about replacing your team.
It’s about making sure inbound doesn’t leak when volume spikes, the phones stack up, or calls come in after hours.

### "We don’t need AI."
Understood.
The issue usually isn’t whether it’s AI.
The issue is whether missed calls and delayed callbacks are costing inspections.
If they are, this is worth looking at.

### "We call people back quickly already."
That may be true.
The real test is what happens when volume spikes and the office gets overloaded.
That’s where leakage usually shows up.

### "Send me something."
Happy to.
What’s better — a short summary or a quick demo link so you can see exactly how it works?
