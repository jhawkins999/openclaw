# Roofing Answer Demo Outline

## Goal

Show roofing companies how Answer helps reduce missed-call revenue leakage during storm-driven demand spikes.

## Demo Structure (10-15 minutes)

### 1. Start with the problem
- storm activity spikes call volume
- office staff gets overloaded
- callbacks slow down
- missed calls become missed inspections

### 2. Show the workflow
- inbound call answered
- lead details captured
- inspection / next step booked
- instant text follow-up sent
- recap routed to the team

### 3. Show why that matters
- first response often wins in roofing
- one recovered job can pay for months of service
- helps absorb surge without adding immediate payroll pressure

### 4. Explain practical fit
- after-hours coverage
- overflow coverage
- storm surge response
- front-office support

### 5. Call to action
- offer a small pilot
- offer setup walkthrough
- offer ROI conversation based on missed-call volume
