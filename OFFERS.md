## OFFER 1 — Founder Bottleneck Diagnostic + 30-Day Execution Sprint

### Positioning
This is not generic coaching. It is a focused operator-led intervention for founder-led companies where growth has outpaced leadership structure.

### Core Problem Solved
- founder bottleneck / decision dependency
- execution drift
- weak leadership team capacity
- growth complexity without matching operating structure

### Core Promise
I help founder-led companies remove the leadership and execution bottlenecks that keep the founder trapped at the center of the business.

### Best-Fit Buyer
- founder-led company
- generally $3M-$15M revenue
- founder still heavily involved in key decisions
- leadership team exists but does not fully carry execution
- growth feels heavier than it should

### Deliverables
#### Diagnostic
- founder bottleneck assessment
- decision flow review
- leadership capacity analysis
- execution drag diagnosis
- priority and accountability review

#### 30-Day Sprint
- one primary bottleneck selected and attacked
- weekly strategic working sessions
- decision pressure-testing
- execution reset and operating cadence improvements
- leadership accountability recommendations
- Force Multiplier integration where useful

### Outcome
- faster decisions
- less founder dependency
- clearer priorities
- stronger execution consistency
- better leadership carry capacity

### Pricing Tests
- Diagnostic only: $3,500
- Diagnostic + 30-Day Sprint: $10,000

### Longer-Term Expansion
- 90-Day Founder Growth Partnership
- Embedded Growth Architect tier
